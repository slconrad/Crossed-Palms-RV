Local Favorites

Minutes from Adventure

From world-class fishing to historic downtown strolls, everything you love about North-East Texas is right outside our gate.

5 Mins Away

Lake Lavon

One of the largest lakes in North Texas. Perfect for high-tier fishing, boating, and sunset views at Clear Lake Park.

12 Mins Away

Historic Farmersville

Walk the iconic Chaparral Rail Trail or enjoy local shopping and dining in the beautifully preserved Main Street district.

15 Mins Away

In-Sync Exotics

A world-renowned big cat sanctuary in Wylie. A must-visit for families and animal lovers staying in the area.

20 Mins Away

The Harbor Rockwall

Looking for a night out? Enjoy lakeside dining, cinema, and live music at this premier entertainment destination.
